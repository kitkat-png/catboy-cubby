Recently I figured out something new about my gender stuff! Turns out I'm not bigender but mostly a guy, I'm bigender as in both a guy and a girl. I'm both boyflux and girlflux which means my feelings of being a guy and a girl both fluctuate!

I'm really excited about this because this realization really explains a lot and it's one more step towards expressing my true self more! Like, turns out I experience gender dysphoria being called a girl because when people call me that they're usually not recognizing my masculine gender at all and they're seeing me as just a girl. I also experience a bit of gender dysphoria being called a guy too when I know people are seeing me as just a guy. I used to not understand why I'd experience dysphoria being called a guy but now I fully get that, yeah, that's really not the whole picture!

I hope everyone has a lovely day today by the way!
